Thanks for installing 3D MAZE!

This is an instruction manual provided with the game.

Launch:

    Click on the start menu entry under '3D Maze' called 'Launch'

Controls:

    W - Go forwards
    A - Turn left
    S - Go backwards
    D - Turn right

To Uninstall:

    Click on the start menu entry under '3D Maze' called 'Uninstall'

Thanks for playing!
